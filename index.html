HACT Programmatic Monitoring Visits Workflow

Step 1: HACT-focused Status Dissemination (CO level)
  • MO prepares and distributes HACT PMV requirements.
  • Distributes due monitoring action points.
  • Disseminates key messages on patterns/quality.
  • Targets relevant CO and Field Office focal points.
MO +PMO +FMfp (PMR oversight)

Timeframe: 3 (monthly cycle)

Output: Required PMV List, Action Points, and HACT Key Messages disseminated.
↓
Step 2: Actions Closure & Field Visits Planning + Submitting Clearance Requests & Admin Work
  • Close or reschedule due monitoring actions.
  • Identify and plan FM visits (programme-focused) and assurance PMVs (partner-focused).
  • Assign Monitoring Lead (ML).
  • Prepare logistics, itineraries; assign Third-Party Monitors (TPM) if needed.
  • ML submits clearance requests.
  • ML adds detailed monitoring plans (dates, partners, monitors, questions, methods) to FMM per site.
Chief PO PMO/FMfp ML& Ms

Timeframe: 5

Output: Pending actions closed/rescheduled; Monitoring Plan added to FMM; Cleared Itinerary Plan.
↓
Step 3: Field Mission & Data Collection, Reporting to FMM, Reviewing & Consolidating Report
  • ML and Ms conduct on-site verification (observation, discussions).
  • Capture notes, findings, evidence; initiate on-spot actions.
  • Ms enter findings (Likert/Options, notes, photos/docs) and agreed partner actions into FMM.
  • ML reviews, consolidates, revises inputs, submits report for QA.
ML +Ms

Timeframe: 5

Output: Monitors' inputs and evidence added to FMM; Reviewed, consolidated, and actionable MV Report submitted for QA.
↓
Step 4: QA Review
  • Conduct cross-sectoral QA review.
  • Use checklist: field methods, safeguarding, verifiability, actionability.
PMO MO

Timeframe: 2

Output: Reviewed PMV Report (QA Status: “Passed” or “Revisions Required”).
↓
Step 5: Revision & Resubmission
  • ML reflects QA reviews.
  • Revises report and actions.
  • Resubmits for QA confirmation.
ML

Timeframe: 2

Output: Revised, Quality-Assured PMV Report; Recorded Action Ownership & Due Dates.
↓
Step 6: Completion
  • PMO/MO marks MV as completed.
  • System notifies ML & Ms.
PMO MO

Timeframe: 2

Output: HACT / FM Requirements Met.
